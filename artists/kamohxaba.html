Kamoh Xaba

Amapiano Artist

About

Kamohelo Xaba, professionally known as Kamoh Xaba, is an exceptionally talented rising star in the Amapiano music scene. Hailing from Qwaqwa, Free State, South Africa, Kamohâ€™s love for music started at a young age, as he was captivated by the rhythms of hip hop and house music. He began his musical journey in 2017 as a rapper and producer, making waves in his township while working with groups like The Mafia Clic. Over time, he transitioned to Amapiano, discovering his versatility and ability to blend various musical styles. His sound is recognized for its spiritual melodies fused with groovy drum patterns.Kamoh has collaborated with several esteemed artists, including Sol Phenduka, Soa Mattrix, Mashudu, Heavy K, DJ Couza, Peekay Mzee, Khathapillar, and Gugu. His collaborative nature and innovative production style have earned him widespread recognition in the industry.His latest single, "Motoaitoai," alongside Khathapillar and Romeo Makota, showcases his mastery of combining Sesotho lyrics with Amapiano instrumentals, further solidifying his position as a forward-thinking artist.
