About

Linda Mnisi, known by the stage name LeeMckrazy, is a vocalist born in Diepkloof Zone 5, Soweto, South Africa.His musical journey began at the age of five in Sunday school at a local church in Soweto. He developed a passion for music after getting inspiration from artists such as Buhlebendalo Mda and the popular amapiano vocalist, Samthing Soweto. In 2015, he started getting involved in the music scene by singing in various choirs which led him to become a member of the renowned Diepkloof United Male Voices group.
